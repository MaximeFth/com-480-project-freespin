Few Words About Our Project

Description

The NBA records and keeps track of all kind of statistics and information about players. You can't watch a single NBA game without seeing a statistic about a player, a record being approached, broken or set. Ever wanted to analyse the performance of a player, his contribution to the different statistics of this game ? Is he a scorer, Someone who makes histeammates shine ? A beast ready to protect his rim at all cost ? All kind of characteristic showing its impact on the game and inside his team are obviously very interesting in such a sport, for franchises fans to rookies discovering the world of basketballs ! Or maybe you're interested in seeing a more geographical aspect of the NBA ? Being the national league in the USA, NBA is always changing, each season rookies make their first step, players retire and rosters change. Focusing on a player, you can follow his journey in the league and how he did or is doing during his career !

How to use those visualizations :

For the sankey diagram, the left one, you can choose the player/players that interest you, sorted by team and years. Just simply click the box to select it/them and move on to the statistics part on your right. There, we offer you the choice of more than 25 categories of statistics, but don't forget that some of them weren't recorded in the oldest days of the NBA and thus, we may not have all the informations about all players. Once you've decided what you wanted to see, just click on "Make Sankey" and let the magic happen ! For the Map, on the right below the statistics, you'll find a scrolling menu with the players you selected for the diagram. Just select one and click on "DRAW!" to see where your picked player played during his career, you can choose any player you picked for making your sankey diagram ! Don't hesitate to hover over our map to find how much players played in a given state!
